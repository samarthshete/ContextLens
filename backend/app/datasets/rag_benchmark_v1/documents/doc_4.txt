Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) combines document retrieval with language model generation. The system retrieves relevant chunks and feeds them into a language model to produce an answer.

RAG systems can fail when retrieval is incomplete or when the model misinterprets the context. Even if retrieval succeeds, the model may hallucinate or omit critical information.

Effective RAG design requires balancing retrieval quality, prompt design, and model behavior.
